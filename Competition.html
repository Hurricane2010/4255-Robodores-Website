MadTown Throwdown



Madtown Throwdown is an offseason competition hosted by team 1323 and 1671 annually at Mathilda Joseph High School in Madera, CA. Teams are allowed to bring multiple robots to the event. In 2022, our team brought its competition season robot, Tropical Storm, to compete with 45 other robots at the event. Competitions like Madtown are used to train rookie members on what to do at competitions and how to act there. The challanges at these off-season events are also the same as the last on-season challange.
